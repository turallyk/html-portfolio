The Best Movies I Have Ever Seen

Top 3

  1. Fight Club (1999)

    An insomniac office worker and a devil-may-care soapmaker form an underground fight club that evolves into something much, much more. This film challenges the norms of society and takes the viewer on a journey through a tangled psyche.

  2. 13 Going on 30 (2004)

    A comedy that brings a fresh twist to the wish-fulfillment fantasy, "13 Going on 30" tells the story of a preteen girl who dreams of being popular. After her thirteenth birthday, she wakes up as a thirty-year-old woman, exploring the unforeseen consequences of her wishes.

  3. The Goldfinch (2019)

    Based on the Pulitzer Prize-winning novel, "The Goldfinch" is a story of survival and self-discovery. Following a boy who survives a terrorist bombing at an art museum, the film delves into themes of grief, guilt, and the tangled web of life's experiences.
